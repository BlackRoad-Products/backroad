01 — Features
Social Without Surveillance
Own your audience. Schedule your content. Measure what matters. No middleman.
S

Scheduled Posts

Write once, schedule everywhere. Queue posts across platforms with timezone-aware scheduling, optimal send-time suggestions, and batch uploads. Never miss a window again.

A

Post Analytics

Real-time performance metrics on every post. Impressions, clicks, shares, saves, and sentiment — all without third-party trackers. Your data stays on your road.

I

Audience Insights

Understand who follows you and why. Demographic breakdowns, growth trends, peak activity hours, and follower cohort analysis — all computed locally, never sold.

X

Cross-Post from Products

Publish directly from RoadBook, BlackBoard, or any BlackRoad product. One click turns a blog post, lesson, or project update into a social post with hashtags and formatting.
